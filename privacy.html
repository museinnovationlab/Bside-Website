B-Side ("B-Side," "we," "us," or "our"), operated by [Berkeley Labs / legal entity name], provides a music-discovery application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the Service you agree to this Policy.

1. Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Account information — your email address and authentication details when you create an account.
  • Taste preferences — the artists, genres, and feedback (loved / fine / not for me) you give us to shape your recommendations.
  • Support communications — anything you send us by email or in-app.

Information from connected music services

When you connect Spotify, Apple Music, or Last.fm, or upload your exported listening history, we access listening-related data such as your top artists, play history, and genres — solely to build your taste profile and recommendations. We request the minimum access needed, and you can disconnect a service at any time. Where applicable, third-party access tokens are stored securely and are not exposed to other users.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage and device data — app interactions, device type, and diagnostics, collected through analytics and crash-reporting tools (currently [PostHog, Sentry]) to improve and stabilize the Service.
  • Subscription data — purchase and subscription status, processed through our payments partner (currently [RevenueCat + Apple App Store / Google Play]). We do not receive or store your full payment-card details.

2. How we use your information

  • To generate and personalize your weekly recommendations and discovery features.
  • To create, maintain, and secure your account and subscription.
  • To send service-related notifications (e.g. "your new radar is ready"), subject to your notification settings.
  • To analyze, debug, and improve the Service.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use your listening data for third-party advertising.

3. How we share information

We share information only with:

  • Service providers that operate the app on our behalf (e.g. [Google Firebase for hosting/auth/database, analytics, crash reporting, payments]), bound to handle data on our instructions.
  • Music services you connect, only as needed to read the data you authorize.
  • Legal and safety recipients when required by law or to protect rights and safety.
  • Business transfers — in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to this Policy.

4. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. Raw uploaded files (such as a listening-history export) are deleted after processing. You can delete your account at any time from the app, after which we delete or de-identify your personal data within [retention period, e.g. 30 days], except where retention is required by law.

5. Your rights and choices

  • Access, correction, deletion — manage your data in-app or request it at [privacy@bside.app]. Account deletion removes your data as described above.
  • Disconnect a service — revoke any connected music service at any time in the app and in that service's own settings.
  • Notifications — control push notifications in your device and app settings.
  • Regional rights — depending on where you live (e.g. EEA/UK under GDPR, or California under the CCPA/CPRA), you may have additional rights such as data portability or the right to object. Contact us to exercise them. [Confirm applicable frameworks with counsel.]

6. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's privacy

The Service is not directed to children under [13 / 16], and we do not knowingly collect their personal information. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

8. International users

We operate from [country/region] and may process your information there and in other countries where our providers operate. By using the Service, you consent to such transfers, handled consistent with this Policy.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the app or by email, and the "Last updated" date above will change.

10. Contact us

Questions about this Policy or your data? Email [privacy@bside.app] or write to [Berkeley Labs, mailing address].
